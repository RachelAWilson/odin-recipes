Spaghetti Recipe

Description

This spaghetti recipe will knock your socks off!
You'll devour it before it has time to fully cool.
Sprinkling some freshly grated parmesan would go perfectly with this timeless recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs ground beef
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 beef bouillon cube
  • 2 16 oz cans marinara of choice
  • Dash of cayenne pepper
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1 Tbsp sugar
  • 1 box of spaghetti noodles
  • 1 cup freshly grated parmesan

Steps

  1. Brown ground beef in large deep skillet
  2. Pour in 1/4 cup water and add bouillon cube
  3. Make sure bouillon dissolves
  4. Pour in marinara sauce
  5. Add cayenne, onion powder, basil, and sugar
  6. Simmer sauce for several hours
  7. Right before serving, cook noodles according to box instructions
  8. Plate noodles first, pour over sauce, top with freshly grated parmesan
