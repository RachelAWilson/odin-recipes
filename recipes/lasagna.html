Lasagna Recipe

Description

This lasagna recipe will knock your socks off!
You'll devour it before it has time to fully cool.
A little garnish of parsley and parmesan would go perfectly with this timeless recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs ground beef
  • 1 diced white onion
  • 2 tablespoons minced garlic
  • 1 beef bouillon dissolved in 1/4 water (makes a really strong broth)
  • 1 Tbsp butter
  • 24 oz tomato sauce of choice
  • salt to taste
  • pepper to taste
  • 1/2 tsp italian seasoning
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 10 cooked lasagna noodles (only 9 needed but 1 extra just incase)
  • 16 oz cottage cheese
  • 16 oz ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella
  • 1.5 cup shredded parmesan

Steps

  1. Brown ground beef in butter
  2. Toss in onion until translucent
  3. Add garlic, wait 1 minute
  4. Add broth, tomato sauce, italian seasoning, and sugar
  5. Add salt and pepper to taste
  6. Set aside
  7. In large bowl, combine cottage cheese, ricotta cheese, egg, mozzarella, and 1 cup parmesan
  8. In greased 9x13 casserole dish, layer 1/2 cup meat sauce, then 3 noodles, then 1/3 of the cheese mixture.
  9. Repeat layers 3 times
  10. Top with remaining parmesan
  11. Cover and bake at 375ËšF for 45 minutes
